White Water Rafting

Mission: Our mission is to deliver safe and enjoyable rafting adventures that exceed our customers' expectations, while maintaining the highest standards of safety and professionalism. We aim to create lifelong memories and foster a deep appreciation for nature and the outdoors.

History

The White Water Rafting Company was founded in 1987 by a group of adventurers who wanted to create a company that would take people on wild rides down treacherous rapids. Despite skepticism and ridicule, the company outfitted their rafts with advanced technology and safety gear and recruited skilled guides. Their reputation grew, and they became a global brand with operations in dozens of countries. The company remains committed to providing exhilarating rafting experiences and pushing the boundaries of adventure tourism.
