Overview

Purpose

Having an online presence is important for a rafting business as it helps increase visibility, attract potential customers, and engage with them through digital marketing efforts. The online presence allows the business to showcase its services, gather customer data, and tailor marketing campaigns to the target audience. The main purpose is to increase brand awareness, attract new customers, and ultimately grow the business.

Audience

The target audience for our water rafting website is adventure seekers and outdoor enthusiasts who are interested in experiencing the thrill of white water rafting. Our ideal customers are individuals or groups between the ages of 18-45 who are physically fit, have an appetite for adventure, and are looking for a unique and exciting experience. Scenario questions: 1. Are you looking for an adrenaline-fueled adventure with your friends or family? 2. Do you enjoy exploring the great outdoors and experiencing the natural beauty of the world around you? 3. Are you interested in trying out new activities and pushing your limits with an exciting and challenging activity such as white water rafting?
