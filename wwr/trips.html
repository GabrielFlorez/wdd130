Trip Descriptions

Multi-Day Wilderness Rafting Trip

This is a multi-day rafting trip that takes you through a remote wilderness area with breathtaking scenery and challenging rapids. You'll be fully immersed in nature and have the opportunity to hike to hidden waterfalls and explore ancient ruins along the way. The trip is fully guided and includes all meals and camping gear.

Half-Day Whitewater Rafting Trip

This trip is a half-day adventure that takes you through thrilling Class III and IV rapids on a scenic river. You'll experience the excitement of navigating rapids and enjoy the beautiful scenery of the surrounding area. The trip is guided by experienced guides and all necessary equipment is provided.

International Rafting Expedition

This trip takes you on a multi-day journey through a foreign country with breathtaking scenery and challenging rapids. You'll experience the local culture and cuisine while paddling through the stunning landscape. The trip is fully guided and includes all meals and equipment./p>
